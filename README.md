## ⭐ Selected research

### 🔍 [LLM-text detection under iterative paraphrasing](https://github.com/razon1494/Analysis-of-Human-vs.-LLM-Generated-Text)

![Research](https://img.shields.io/badge/Research-LLM%20Detection-6F42C1?style=flat-square)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Manuscript%20in%20Preparation-F59E0B?style=flat-square)
![Code](https://img.shields.io/badge/Code-Reproducible-2EA44F?style=flat-square)

A reproducible study of character-TF-IDF, word-TF-IDF, and RoBERTa detectors under iterative standard and simplified paraphrasing.

> **Key result:** On a readability-defined hard subset, character-TF-IDF F1 decreases from **0.923 to 0.336** after two simplified paraphrasing rounds.

### 🔐 [Membership-inference probing in NLP](https://github.com/razon1494/membership-inference-probing-nlp)

Investigating whether paraphrase perturbations erase training-data membership signals in language models through behavioral and probability-based analyses.

### 🌐 [Prompt learning under domain shift](https://github.com/razon1494/clip-prompt-domain-shift)

Evaluating prompt learning, zero-shot inference, and linear probing with CLIP across PACS and OfficeHome domain shifts.
